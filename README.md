# HR-Analytics-Dashboard
1.Project Title
HR Analytics Dashboard- Employee Attrition Insights (Power BI)

2. Short Descripation
   Build an interactive HR Analytics dashboard built using Power BI to analyze employee attrition trends. The dashboard provides insights into key factors such as age, salary, education, job role, and years at the company, helping organizations make data-driven HR decisions and improve employee retention.

3.Tech Stack
The key technologies used to build the dashboard
Power BI – Data visualization & dashboard development
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculated measures & KPIs
Power Query (M Language) – Data cleaning & transformation
Excel / CSV Dataset – Data source
Data Modeling – Relationships & schema design

4. Features/Highlights
Business Problem:
Organizations face high employee attrition, leading to increased hiring costs, productivity loss, and workforce instability. There is a need to identify key drivers of attrition and patterns across different employee segments.

Goal of the Dashboard:
To provide a comprehensive and interactive view of employee attrition, enabling HR teams to identify trends, understand root causes, and take data-driven actions to improve retention.

Walkthrough of Key Visuals:
Age Analysis: Highlights which age group is most likely to leave
Salary Slab Analysis: Shows correlation between salary and attrition
Job Role Analysis: Identifies roles with highest turnover
Tenure Trend: Reveals when employees are most likely to leave
Education Analysis: Helps understand attrition based on background

Insights:
KPI cards showing Total Employees, Attrition Count, Attrition Rate, Avg Salary, Avg Age, Avg Tenure
Attrition breakdown by:
Age groups 
Salary slabs 
Education fields 
Job roles 
Gender
Attrition trend by Years at Company 
Department-level filtering for focused insights
